# Daily Flow - Cycle Companion ✨

A gentle, personalized companion app that adapts to every phase of your menstrual cycle with customized daily schedules, workout routines, nutrition guidance, and self-care reminders.

## Features

- **Cycle-Phase Adaptive Schedules**: Different schedules for each phase (Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulatory, Luteal)
- **Personalized Gym Plans**: Weekly workout schedule with phase-appropriate modifications
- **Nutrition Guidance**: Portion control and meal suggestions based on your phase
- **Progress Tracking**: Save and resume your daily progress
- **Beautiful UI**: Hand-crafted design with soothing colors that change with your cycle phase
- **Mobile-First**: Responsive design works on all devices
- **PWA Support**: Install on your phone for offline access

## How to Use

1. Open the app
2. Enter your current cycle day (1-28)
3. The app auto-detects today's day of the week
4. Follow the gentle guidance throughout your day
5. Mark tasks as completed or skip them - no pressure!
